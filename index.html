Code for Princeton

Code for Princeton is a group of techies and community members interested in open government and civic hacking. We host hack nights, workshops, and talks plus work with the municipal government to provide open datasets. If you’re a techie, work in local government, or are a community member interested in civic engagement, this group is for you!
